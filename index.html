Optimal EV Spread Calculator v1.2

This calculator is made to optimize the damage taken from a specific attack from a specific Pokémon.

For example, an attack from a Special Attacker will do more damage to you if you invest all of your remaining EV's into HP, than if you were to invest some of your EV's into Special Defense. But how much should I invest to take the least damage?

That is what this tool determines! Select the attacker, target, move, and any other modifiers. The tool will determine the optimal HP-Defense split.

(Note: The first time the webpage loads per device, it can take up to a few minutes to fetch all of the required data. After that, it should load faster.)
